Mannbassadors:

The full list of Mannbassadors, as well as the coordinates used for their locations, can be found here. While most Mannbassadors are placed where you would expect (i.e. the point for David from Christchurch is placed in Christchurch) some of the more unconventional jurisdictions required special placement. They are:

Name: Jurisdiction: Location on the Map:
Fuzz Music Ugarit, Syria, location of the oldest, surviving, substantially complete piece of written music.
Rip Babyboomers The headquarters of the American Association of Retired Persons in Washington DC.
Aidan The RAF RAF Brize Norton
Sister Shelly The NHS St. Thomas's Hospital in London.
Phil Tottenham Hotspur Tottenham Hotspur's stadium.
Nick Under the bridge at the South Bank It's unclear specifically what bridge is referred to, but absent further information the Hungerford and Golden Jubilee Bridges have been used.
Charlie Charlie Location chosen for approximate location of the Clair Oil Field.

If any of you feel a different location would better represent your jurisdiction, please get in touch!
